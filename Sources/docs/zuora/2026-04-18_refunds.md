# Refunds — 공식 원문

> 2026-04-18 수집. Zuora Payments > Process payments > Manage payment processing > Refunds.

## Overview

Zuora allows you to **issue and track refunds on payments**. Similar to external payments, users can enter external refunds to track refunds that have been performed outside of Zuora Payments (for example, by issuing a check). In addition, you can make electronic refunds using our supported payment gateways, which will automatically refund money to the customer.

## Referenced Refunds

A referenced refund is **linked to the original payment**. Regardless of using Zuora UI or API, the referenced refund can only be processed by using:

- **Original payment method**, with which the payment was processed
- **Original payment gateway**, through which the payment was processed

If the original payment gateway is **deactivated**, the reference refund cannot be processed successfully and an error will return.

If you plan to change your current payment gateway:
1. Keep the current payment gateway active and continue using it to process referenced refunds for the existing payments.
2. With the new payment gateway, create **non-referenced refunds** for the payments processed through the current payment gateway.

If the account and payment method data at the gateway side have been migrated to your new merchant account, the refund will fail on the current gateway even though the current gateway is still active. In this case, you must use option 2.

## Non-Referenced Refunds

Non-referenced refunds, also known as **Credit Balance Refunds**, are not directly associated with a specific payment. Non-referenced refunds do not have to be issued to the original payment method or through a specific payment gateway.

A non-referenced refund is a refund transaction that does not use the credit card information from an existing payment transaction. If you cannot obtain the customer's credit card information, another option is to issue a manual check using your Accounts Payable process. When performing a refund outside of Zuora, remember to reflect that refund within Zuora as an **external refund**.

Non-referenced refunds are available only if you have enabled the **Credit Balance** feature.

> Note: If you have **Invoice Settlement enabled, credit balance is no longer supported.** So the non-referenced refunds are **refunds on credit memos**. See Refund Credit Memos.

## How long to refund

Zuora 내부는 제한 없음. 그러나 **electronic payment는 gateway 정책에 종속**:
- 일반적으로 90일 이내
- 게이트웨이마다 다름 (30일 제한도 있음)
- API로 하는 refund와 virtual terminal 하는 refund의 제한이 다를 수 있음
- 기간 초과 시 gateway 에러 반환 (예: PayPal "10009 - You are over the time limit...")

## Troubleshooting

**Refunds in processing status after trial balance run**: 같은 account에 동시 refund 생성 시 gateway가 approved 보내도 "processing" 으로 남음. 통합 로직에서 동시 refund 생성 방지 필요.

# Refund Credit Memos — 공식 원문 (Invoice Settlement 맥락)

URL: `https://docs.zuora.com/en/zuora-billing/bill-your-customer/adjust-invoice-amounts/invoice-settlement/credit-memos-and-debit-memos/management-of-debit-and-credit-memos/refund-credit-memos`

## Overview

Learn how to issue full or partial refunds for credit memos, configure refund rules, and understand the implications of refund dates.

- **Posted credit memos**에 대해 full/partial refund 가능 (권한 필요)
- Credit memo가 fully refunded 되면 **더 이상 credit memo part를 포함하지 않음**
- **Application rule** 설정으로 refund 방식 제어
- REST API: [POST Refund Credit Memo](https://developer.zuora.com/v1-api-reference/api/operation/POST_RefundCreditMemo/)

## Notes — Refund date와 EffectiveDate 차이

When you refund a credit memo, the **refund date may not equal** the RefundApplication.EffectiveDate and RefundApplicationItem.EffectiveDate.

The RefundApplication.EffectiveDate and RefundApplicationItem.EffectiveDate will **equal or later than the effective date of the last apply or unapply action** on the credit memo.

Example: refund on 3/29, but last apply on credit memo was 3/30 → RefundApplication.EffectiveDate = 3/30 (not 3/29).
