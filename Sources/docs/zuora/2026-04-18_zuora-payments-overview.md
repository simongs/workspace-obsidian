# Zuora Payments overview — 공식 문서 원문

> 2026-04-18 수집. Overview 페이지가 매우 짧음 (인트로 + 이미지 한 장 수준).

## 공식 본문

> Zuora Payments provides an end-to-end solution for managing invoices, payment gateways, and automated collections.
>
> Zuora Payments is an end-to-end payment solution. It offers a simple way to turn invoices into cash with varied preferences for pre-built payment gateways. It can also manage automated payment processes and optimize recurring collections with automated dunning and machine learning.
